O&M

We use state-of-the-art technology to operate and maintain photovoltaic plants in the PMGD segment, maximizing their performance and efficiency. We have an interdisciplinary team of around 60 people that today operate and maintain approximately 60 of oEnergy’s own plants plants and those of strategic clients.

Operations

We operate solar plants 100% remotely. Our IT team developed our own customized interface for monitoring their electrical variables based on the general-purpose SCADA system (Ignition).

Maintenance

We carry out preventative and corrective maintenance, which is planned and executed regularly and systematically. Our processes seek to maximize the performance and efficiency of the solar plants, ensuring continuous production of clean and sustainable energy.

Asset Assurance

We have a team of operators who control access and monitor remotely our plants 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. We develop special procedures for the detection of intruders, deterrence, and escalation to local emergency services in close collaboration with neighboring communities.

Integrated surveillance, detection, and deterrence system

The surveillance, detection, and deterrence systems (CCTV, lighting, image analytics, etc.) are integrated based on our customization of the Ignition technological platform, developed by our IT team. This allows the visualization and monitoring of intrusion variables on one control panel.

Quality standard of our services

ASSET MANAGEMENT

Our team dedicated exclusively to asset management is in charge of the administration and commercialization of the photovoltaic plants in order to maximize income from energy and power sales. We provide accounting, technical, and regulatory advice, being counterparts of the National Electricity Coordinator.
